Chicago Public Schools Teacher Tele-Townhall May 17, 2012

CPS Tele-Townhall Conference Call with the Chicago Public Schools Leadership Team for teachers. Topics included Full (Longer) Day , Common Core implementation, recess, sick days, contract negotiations, and more. Thursday May 17, 2012 5 PM - 6 PM.

  1. Chicago Public Schools
    Please join CPS CEO Jean-Claude Brizard and members of his Leadership Team on Thursday, May 17, from 5 to 6 p.m., for another opportunity to ask questions live via phone about issues you care about – from the Full School Day and the new teacher evaluation system to the implementation of the Common Core State Standards, among other topics. Call 1-888-886-6603 and when prompted, punch in 18778 and then the # sign from any phone. Thanks in advance for participating in this call!
