CPS Public Hearing: Best Practice High School

A hearing for the technical closing of Best Practice High School. This school has already been phased out and no longer has students. Held on the 15th floor of the Chicago Public Schools administration building. Wednesday, January 25th, 2012 5:30 - 7:30 PM. 4 public speakers.
